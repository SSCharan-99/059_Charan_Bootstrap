Brief Description about the projects:

Modified Asphalt Using CNT:

In a developing nation like India, construction and improvement of quality roads plays a prominent role. Our aim is to increase the strength properties of a pavement by using Nanotechnology. Nanotechnologies are a quickly extending territory of research in highway infrastructure. Carbon Nano Tubes are basic carbon consisting of curved graphene layer which consists of single layer of atoms in combined hexagonal structure. Carbon Nanotubes improve the mechanical properties of concrete. Carbon Nanotube is a very efficient reinforcing agent due to its extremely high aspect ratio and ultra-high strength. In this study, a parametric experimental investigation was undertaken to determine optimum mix dosage of CNT in Concrete and in Asphalt or bitumen.



Tic Tac Toe:

Tic-Tac-Toe, a two player game, is one of the most played games and is the best time killer game since childhood. We can play that game with just a pen and paper. Inspired by that game, we decided to create that game on the computer using GUI which Graphical user Interface. The game is to be played between two people One of the players chooses ‘O’ and the other ‘X’ to mark their respective cells. The game starts with one of the players and the game ends when one of the players has one whole row/ column/ diagonal filled with his/her respective character (‘O’ or ‘X’). If no one wins, then the game is said to be drawn.
